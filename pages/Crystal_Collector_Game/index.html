Welcome to the crystal mines of Gileador!

You have been contracted by the king to collect magic gems, each of which change in power whenever you enter the mines... and in some cases, when touched. With the enchanted stash-bag you've been given, try to match the Gems Amount to the Bag Size exactly.

Due to the spell on the stash-bag, it will change size each time you enter the mines -- careful not to overfill, or it will rip!

Be wary of black crystals and white crystals -- though they have their own uses, black crystals can quickly fill your stash-bag to bursting, and white crystals will shrink others you have collected... but can only be used sparingly.
